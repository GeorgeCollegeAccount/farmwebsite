Our Story

Back in 1889 the farmer you see in front of you, Farmer McFarmerson, he realised that farming was really fun but what would be more fun if there was other people to hang out with. So to start with he made it a petting zoo for his friends kids to come and visit and then through the years opened it up to the public adding more things to do.

This is who runs the farm now and is the great grandson of Farmer McFarmerson wants the same goal of letting people have fun at the farm.
